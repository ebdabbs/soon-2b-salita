Meet the Bridal Party

Maid of Honor

Amy puts the party in bridal party. As the youngest in the family, Amy is always there to make sure we know what's hip. Helen Salita

Sister of the Groom

Helen and Lizzie would have been friends even if Helen wasn't Michael's little sister. In high school, they both played clarinet and marched cymbals together. Cody Handshaw

High School Friend

Cody attended Longwood University and is currently working in northern Virginia.
